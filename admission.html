Who Can Enroll:

At least high school graduates, 18 years old and above, who want to pursue flying as a profession Professionals and hobbyists who want to take up flying as a profession or as a leisurely pursuit Professional pilots who need recurrency and/or additional ratings Before we proceed with your training, please furnish us a copy of the following documents:

For Filipino citizens:

- Transcript of records and diploma for graduates from another college or course
- Original NBI Clearance
- NSO Birth Certificate:

For Foreign Students:

- Valid passport
- Authenticated copies of Visa 9 (f) from country of origin
- Original NBI Clearance
- Alien Certificate of Registration Identity Card (can be applied at the Philippine’s Bureau of Immigration):
